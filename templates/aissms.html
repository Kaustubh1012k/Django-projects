AISSMS College of Engineering is a private engineering college located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. The college is affiliated with the University of Pune. It was founded by Chhatrapati Shri Shahu Maharaj of Kolhapur, leading to the college's establishment in 1992. The institute is located close to the Regional Transport Office. It shares its campus with a pharmacy college, Polytechnic and business school. At present, AISSMS offers bachelor's degrees in eight branches of engineering: NAAC- A+ (3.27 CGPA) & *NBA ACCREDITATION*
