Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune, India. One of the great attractions of Pune. It was the great seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire. The fort itself was largely destroyed in 1828 by an unexplained fire, but the surviving structures are now maintained as a tourist site.
