Appa Balwant Chowk or ABC known as the open street library of Pune. Situated at NC Kelkar Road, in Budhwar Peth. The crossroad has book stores - big and small, at every possible nook-and-corner. Competitive exam books to bestsellers, recipe books to literature, whatever it is that you are seeking, you shall find at ABC at wholesale rates. A must visit place for booklovers.
