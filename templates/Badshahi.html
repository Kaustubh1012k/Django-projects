Badshahi is a quaint humble food joint with stone tile flooring, erstwhile wooden furniture and old ceiling fans. The price of the thali changes daily, depending on the veg curry or gravy made on that particular day. But on no given day, the price will exceed beyond INR 100. An unlimited thali has rice, phulkas, dry and gravy sabzi, amti, chutney and comes with a glass of buttermilk. Badshahi is a place where you go for the food, the nostalgia and the courteous service. It’s absolute bare-bones, so don’t expect fancy air-conditioned interiors. The thali is yum and freakishly affordable. If you’re looking for a hidden gem in the city, which is true to the Puneri culture, this is the place to be!
