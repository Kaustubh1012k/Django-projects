Situated in the heart of Pune in Shaniwar Peth, the 17th century Omkareshwar Temple is a synonym of the rich Maratha culture and heritage. The temple was built by Chimaji Appa, a commander of the Maratha Empire and Peshwa Bajirao between 1740 to 1760 on the banks of Mutha river. Other than being a shire to Lord Shiva, the temple also has the samadhi of Chimaji Appa which makes this sacred space even more alluring for the visitors.
